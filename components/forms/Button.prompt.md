Square-cornered, bold-label button — the primary call to action across the ARMOR BIKE storefront (Add to cart, Apply filter).

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" size="lg">Add to cart</Button>
<Button variant="sale">Shop the sale</Button>
<Button variant="secondary">More filter</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (brand blue), `accent` (yellow, for high-energy promos), `sale` (magenta), `secondary` (blue outline), `ghost`. Sizes `sm | md | lg`. Use `block` to fill width, `iconLeft` / `iconRight` for icons. Labels are bold and kept short.
